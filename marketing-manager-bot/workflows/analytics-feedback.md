# Analytics Feedback Workflow

## Goal

Use campaign performance data to improve the next outreach and social content cycle.

## Required Inputs

- Email opens
- Email clicks
- Replies
- Bookings or sales conversations
- Social impressions
- Social engagement
- Follower growth
- Lead quality notes

## Steps

1. Analytics Feedback Agent collects performance data.
2. Separate observed results from assumptions.
3. Compare message angles, CTA types, and channels.
4. Identify top-performing and weak-performing content.
5. Recommend improvements for the next week.
6. Update market insights, objections, and campaign notes.

## Metrics

- Email open rate
- Email click rate
- Reply rate
- Booking count
- Post impressions
- Post engagement
- Follower growth
- Lead quality

## Output

```yaml
report_id: ""
period: ""
observed_results: []
interpretation: []
recommended_changes: []
risks_or_limits: []
next_week_priorities: []
```
