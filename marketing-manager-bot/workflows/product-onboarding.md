# Product Onboarding Workflow

## Goal

Convert real owner-provided product information into a campaign-ready foundation.

## Required Inputs

- Completed product entry in `knowledge-base/products.md`
- Audience notes in `knowledge-base/audiences.md`
- Offer facts in `knowledge-base/offers.md`
- Known objections and limits

## Steps

1. Owner adds real product facts.
2. Product Strategist Agent checks completeness and flags missing facts.
3. Product Strategist Agent writes positioning and allowed campaign messages.
4. Swedish Market Research Agent identifies relevant Swedish sectors and channels.
5. Campaign planning skill turns positioning into campaign angles.
6. Compliance Review Agent checks claims and language risks.
7. Human Approval Gate marks the product marketing foundation as owner-review or approved.

## Outputs

- Positioning summary
- Approved audience recommendation
- Allowed claims
- Prohibited claims
- Campaign angle list

## Blockers

- Missing price when price is needed in outreach.
- Missing booking or purchase link when CTA requires it.
- Unsupported claims.
- Owner approval missing.
