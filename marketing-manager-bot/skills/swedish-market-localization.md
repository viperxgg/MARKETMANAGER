# Skill: Swedish Market Localization

## Purpose

Adapt positioning, offers, and messaging to Swedish business expectations.

## Inputs

- Product positioning
- Target sector
- Swedish market notes
- Audience objections
- Channel context

## Method

1. Translate the offer into Swedish business value, not literal words.
2. Remove aggressive sales pressure.
3. Emphasize clarity, trust, simplicity, and customer experience.
4. Check whether the CTA feels low-friction.
5. Note local terms, tone, and channel expectations.
6. Flag claims that need Swedish market support.

## Output Format

```yaml
localization:
  audience_context: ""
  tone_guidance: ""
  localized_value_angle_sv: ""
  recommended_cta_sv: ""
  terms_to_use: []
  terms_to_avoid: []
  research_needed: []
```

## Quality Checklist

- Sounds suitable for Swedish B2B communication.
- Avoids exaggerated commercial language.
- Keeps claims factual.
- Makes the next step simple and respectful.
