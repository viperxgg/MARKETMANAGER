# Skill: Product Positioning

## Purpose

Turn real product facts into a clear, truthful market position.

## Inputs

- Product facts
- Target audience
- Competitor notes
- Customer problem
- Product limits
- Approved proof points

## Method

1. Identify the most specific audience the product can serve well.
2. Define the practical problem the product solves.
3. Name the main value in one clear sentence.
4. List allowed proof points and missing proof.
5. Define what must not be claimed.
6. Produce 2-4 campaign angles for testing.

## Output Format

```yaml
positioning:
  audience: ""
  problem: ""
  value_proposition: ""
  differentiators: []
  allowed_claims: []
  prohibited_claims: []
  campaign_angles: []
  status: "draft"
```

## Quality Checklist

- Based only on real product facts.
- Specific audience, not "everyone".
- No invented proof or promises.
- Easy to translate into Swedish campaign copy.
