# Skill: Swedish B2B Copywriting

## Purpose

Write calm, professional Swedish copy for B2B audiences in Sweden.

## Inputs

- Product facts
- Audience segment
- Offer or campaign angle
- Platform or channel
- Allowed and prohibited claims

## Method

1. Identify the business value in practical terms.
2. Write the message in natural Swedish, not literal translation.
3. Keep the tone respectful, concise, and low-pressure.
4. Focus on customer experience, clarity, trust, and simplicity.
5. Remove unsupported claims, hype, and negative comparisons.
6. Add a soft CTA if the format requires it.

## Output Format

```yaml
copy_sv: ""
cta_sv: ""
tone_notes: ""
claims_used: []
claims_avoided: []
status: "draft"
approved_by_owner: false
```

## Quality Checklist

- Sounds like natural Swedish business writing.
- Does not shame or criticize the target company.
- Does not overpromise.
- Uses a clear and specific value angle.
- Uses only approved product facts.

## Avoid

- "Ni förlorar kunder"
- "Er hemsida är dålig"
- "Vi garanterar fler bokningar"
- Hard-sell urgency
- English phrasing translated word-for-word
