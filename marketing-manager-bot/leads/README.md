# Leads

This folder stores lead drafts, verified contact notes, website analysis notes, and approved lead lists.

## Suggested Files

- `lead-template.md`: Required structure for every lead.
- `draft-leads.md`: Early lead discoveries.
- `verified-leads.md`: Leads with verified contact details.
- `approved-leads.md`: Leads approved by the owner for outreach.

## Lead Rules

- Every lead must have a clear fit reason.
- Every email address must include a source URL.
- Leads cannot be contacted until owner-approved.
- Website analysis must remain respectful and internal.
