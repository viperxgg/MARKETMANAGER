# Outreach Rules

## Draft Structure

Each outreach draft should include:

- Company
- Product
- Subject
- Opening
- Observation
- Opportunity
- Product connection
- Soft CTA
- Closing
- Language
- Tone
- Compliance checklist
- Status
- `approved_by_owner`

## Rules

- Swedish language for Swedish market.
- Natural and human tone.
- No aggressive language.
- No shame-based wording.
- No guaranteed results.
- No fake claims.
- No invented customer data.
- No mass generic templates.
- Include unsubscribe or polite opt-out note when campaign style requires it.

## Status Flow

```text
draft -> compliance_review -> needs_revision -> owner_review -> approved_by_owner -> sent_manually -> replied -> follow_up_needed -> closed
```
