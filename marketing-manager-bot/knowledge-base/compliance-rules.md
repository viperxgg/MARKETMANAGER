# Compliance Rules

## Global Safety Rule

No email, post, schedule, publication, or contact action can happen unless:

```yaml
approved_by_owner: true
manual_execution_required: true
```

Even when approved, the system must not send or publish automatically.

## Outreach Rules

- Use official company contact sources.
- Do not guess email addresses.
- Do not use personal emails unless a clear business reason exists.
- Include a polite opt-out note when campaign style requires it.
- Keep a clear business reason for contact.
- Keep all outreach personalized.

## Claims Rules

- No guaranteed revenue.
- No guaranteed bookings.
- No guaranteed Skatteverket approval.
- No official GDPR certification claim unless documented.
- No fake customer data.
- No invented proof.

## Website Analysis Rule

Website analysis is internal only. Convert findings into opportunity framing before outreach.
