# Social Content Rules

## Supported Platforms

- Facebook
- Instagram
- LinkedIn

## Post Types

- Educational
- Trust-building
- Product demo
- Problem/solution
- Case-study style
- Behind the scenes
- Offer
- FAQ
- Objection handling

## Platform Rules

### Facebook

- Slightly longer
- Explanatory
- Community/trust tone

### Instagram

- Shorter
- More visual
- Stronger hook
- Relevant hashtags

### LinkedIn

- Professional
- Founder or technical credibility
- Business outcome tone

## Visual Prompt Format

- Image concept
- Composition
- Style
- Colors
- Objects
- What to avoid
- English image prompt

## Safety

No auto publishing. Every post remains draft-first and approval-gated.
