# Tone Of Voice

## Swedish Agency Tone

All external Swedish copy must feel like a serious, calm, professional Swedish B2B agency.

## Required Qualities

- Respectful
- Calm
- Professional
- Opportunity-based
- Clear and practical
- Low-pressure
- Culturally suitable for Swedish business communication

## Forbidden Tone

- Direct criticism
- Shame-based wording
- Fear-based claims
- Exaggerated promises
- Aggressive urgency
- Overly salesy language
- Claims that imply guaranteed outcomes

## Preferred Pattern

```text
Det finns en möjlighet att göra kundresan ännu tydligare och enklare.
```

## Avoid

```text
Er hemsida är svag och ni förlorar kunder.
```

## Daily Rule

Before writing a new post or email, check Agency Memory for:

- Winning messages
- Weak messages
- Customer language
- Objections
- Compliance lessons
