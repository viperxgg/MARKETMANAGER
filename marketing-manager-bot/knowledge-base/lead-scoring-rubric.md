# Lead Scoring Rubric

Lead score is a quality score, not a sales guarantee.

## Qualified Threshold

```yaml
fit_score: ">= 85"
```

Leads below 85 must not be marked qualified.

## Rubric

- Product fit: 0-25
- Visible need: 0-25
- Market relevance: 0-20
- Contact confidence: 0-15
- Timing/opportunity: 0-15

Total: 100

## Required Evidence

Each lead must include:

- Company name
- Website
- City/region
- Industry
- Source URL
- Reason for fit
- Website analysis
- Best outreach angle
- Official email
- Email source URL
- Email source page
- Confidence level

## Rejection Rules

Reject a lead if:

- Email is guessed.
- Email source is missing.
- Company fit is generic.
- Website cannot be reviewed.
- Outreach angle would require criticism or pressure.
