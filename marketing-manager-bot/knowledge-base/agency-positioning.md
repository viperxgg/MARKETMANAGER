# Agency Positioning

## Position

An AI-assisted Swedish marketing agency operating system for strategy, lead research, campaign planning, content production, approval control, tracking, learning memory, and professional reporting.

## What The System Does

- Plans campaigns.
- Creates draft content.
- Prepares Codex research prompts.
- Scores leads with a strict quality rubric.
- Stores manual tracking results.
- Turns learning into Agency Memory.
- Produces export-ready reports.

## What The System Does Not Do

- It does not send emails automatically.
- It does not publish social posts automatically.
- It does not contact real leads automatically.
- It does not store API keys in Markdown.
- It does not treat fit score as a sales guarantee.

## Operating Promise

The system helps the owner plan, draft, approve, track, learn, and improve professionally while keeping real-world execution manual and approval-gated.
